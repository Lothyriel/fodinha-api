L = number of lifes
P = number of players
N = number of cards

Turn (1 Card) -> the smallest unit of the game, when a player needs to play a card
Round (P Turns) -> each player deals a card the highest valued one wins the round
Set (N Rounds) -> each round is played and at the end players who missed their bid loose points
Game (~L*P Sets) -> sets keep being played until 1 or 0 winners remain
